Show spam statistics in the footer of your blog.

== Description ==

Show spam statistics on your blog of your own choice. Choose an interval such as "x spam caught last 7 days", where you can change the text to anything you want. show your visitors that you have a blog entirely free of spam comments.

Spam statistics requires the latest version of [SpamTask](http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/spamtask/ "SpamTask").

== Installation ==

1. Simply upload spam-statistics.php to your blog's plugin folder, activate it and you're done.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Why does it require SpamTask? =

SpamTask is the spam filter which provides the necessary spam statistics for your blog.
